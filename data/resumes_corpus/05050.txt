This article describes the study of the use of an interactive simulation designed to help students understand how glacial melting affects sea level rise by using interactives that link glacial contour maps to visualizations of glacial melting and the resulting sea level rise. Students using this simulation reported high engagement scores and performed well on comprehension questions.
